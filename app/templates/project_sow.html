Project SOW

Scope of Work

The "Mobile App Development for Customer Service" project aims to create a mobile application for a telecommunications company to enhance customer engagement and streamline support services. The application will include self-service features such as billing inquiries, account management, service troubleshooting, and live chat integration. The scope is limited to design, development, testing, and deployment of the application, along with integration with the company's existing CRM system. Post-deployment maintenance is excluded from this scope.

Location of Work

The project team will work remotely, using cloud-based collaboration tools (e.g., GitHub, Slack, and Microsoft Teams). Testing and deployment will be conducted on the company's staging servers and live app store platforms (Google Play Store and Apple App Store).

Period of Performance

The project will commence on December 3, 2024, and conclude on February 20, 2025. Key milestones include:

  • Inititation Phase: Dec 3, 2024 - Dec 12, 2024
  • Planning Phase: Dec 13, 2024 - Dec 27, 2025
  • Execution Phase: Dec 27, 2025 - Jan 26, 2025
  • Testing and Deployment: Jan 27, 2025 - Feb 20, 2025

Deliverables Schedule

  • Requirement Specification Document: Dec 12, 2024
  • UI/UX Design Mockups: Dec 23, 2025
  • Fully Functional Mobile App: Jan 26, 2025
  • Test Reports: Feb 10, 2025
  • Deployed App: Feb 20, 2025

Applicable Standards

  • Data Privacy: Compliance with GDPR and CCPA regulations for protecting user data.
  • Security: Adherence to OWASP Mobile Security standards for secure app development.
  • Accessibility: Compliance with WCAG 2.1 guidelines to ensure usability for all users.
  • App Store Guidelines: Adherence to Google Play and Apple App Store submission requirements.

Acceptance Criteria

  • The mobile app must pass all functional, performance, and security tests.
  • All features must align with the approved requirements document.
  • The app must be successfully deployed to both Google Play and Apple App Store.
  • Stakeholder approval of all deliverables before the project conclusion.

Special Requirements

  • The app must integrate seamlessly with the company’s existing CRM system.
  • Data encryption must be implemented for all sensitive information.
  • The app must support high scalability to accommodate peak user loads.
  • Cross-platform compatibility must be ensured for both iOS and Android devices.

Summary

The Statement of Work (SOW) outlines the scope, schedule, deliverables, and standards for the "Mobile App Development for Customer Service" project. This document will serve as the guiding reference to ensure alignment between the project team and the client, ensuring successful project execution and completion within the agreed upon timeline and budget.
