Project Charter

Project Title

Community Park Renovation

Authorization Date

January 10, 2024

Project Manager

Name: [Your Name]

Contact: [Your Email]

Objectives

To renovate the local community park by upgrading the playground equipment, adding a walking trail, and installing shaded picnic areas. The project aims to create a safe, engaging, and eco-friendly space for families and community gatherings.

Business Justification

The current park facilities are outdated and underutilized. Renovating the park will increase community engagement, provide a safe space for children, and promote healthy outdoor activities.

Success Criteria

  • All renovations completed within budget and on time.
  • Playground equipment meets safety standards.
  • Walking trail and picnic areas are fully functional and accessible.
  • Positive feedback from community stakeholders.

Assumptions

  • All materials will be delivered on schedule.
  • Weather conditions will permit construction work.

Constraints

  • Budget capped at $50,000.
  • Renovation must be completed by April 30, 2024.

Roles and Responsibilities

  • Project Manager (You): Oversee all project phases and ensure milestones are met.
  • Civil Engineer: Design and implement the walking trail and equipment layout.
  • Environmental Consultant: Plan eco-friendly landscaping.
  • Contractor: Manage construction and installation tasks.

Communication Standards

Regular updates will be provided via weekly meetings and email reports. Major milestones will be communicated to stakeholders with detailed status reports.
