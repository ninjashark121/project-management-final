Project Charter

Project Title

Mobile App Development for Customer Service

Authorization Date

December 3, 2024

Project Manager

Name: [Your Name]

Contact: [Your Email]

Summary Schedule

The project will begin on December 3, 2024, and is scheduled for completion by March 31, 2025. The major milestones include:

  • Requirement Gathering and Planning: December 3, 2024 - December 15, 2024
  • Design Phase: December 16, 2024 - January 15, 2025
  • Development Phase: January 16, 2025 - February 28, 2025
  • Testing and Deployment: March 1, 2025 - March 31, 2025

Budget

The estimated budget for the project is $100,000, allocated as follows:

  • Development: $50,000
  • Testing: $20,000
  • Deployment: $10,000
  • Customer Support Integration: $20,000

Objectives

The objective is to develop a mobile app that enhances customer service by providing self-service options such as billing inquiries, account management, service troubleshooting, and live chat support. This project aims to improve customer satisfaction and reduce service costs.

Business Justification

The telecommunications company currently faces delays and inefficiencies in addressing customer concerns. The mobile app will streamline service delivery, reduce dependency on customer service representatives, and enhance the overall customer experience.

Success Criteria

  • App development and deployment are completed within budget and on time.
  • All app features, including live chat and troubleshooting, are functional and user-friendly.
  • Customer satisfaction scores improve by at least 20% within the first quarter post-launch.
  • Customer service calls are reduced by 30% after app adoption.

Assumptions

  • The app will comply with security and privacy standards.
  • All required development tools and resources will be available.
  • Stakeholders will provide timely feedback during review phases.

Constraints

  • The budget is limited to $100,000.
  • The app must be deployed by March 31, 2025.
  • Integration with existing customer support systems must not exceed 20% of the overall timeline.

Roles and Responsibilities

  • Project Manager (You): Oversee all project phases, manage budget, and ensure deadlines are met.
  • UI/UX Designer: Create an intuitive and engaging user interface for the app.
  • Mobile App Developers: Code and implement app features, including front-end and back-end functionality.
  • Quality Assurance Team: Test app functionality, performance, and security.
  • Customer Support Specialist: Ensure seamless integration of the app with existing CRM systems.

Communication Standards

Regular updates will be provided through bi-weekly team meetings and detailed progress reports. Stakeholders will receive milestone updates, and urgent issues will be communicated via email or messaging platforms.
