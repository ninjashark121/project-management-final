Project Title

Mobile App Development for Customer Service

Authorization Date

Project Manager

Name: Seth Williams

Schedule and Budget

Summary Schedule

The project will begin on December 3, 2024, and is scheduled for completion by March 31, 2025. Key milestones include:

  • Requirement Gathering and Planning: Dec 3, 2024 - Dec 15, 2024
  • Design Phase: Dec 16, 2024 - Jan 15, 2025
  • Development Phase: Jan 16, 2025 - Feb 28, 2025
  • Testing and Deployment: Mar 1, 2025 - Mar 31, 2025

Budget

The estimated budget is $100,000, allocated as follows:

  • Development: $50,000
  • Testing: $20,000
  • Deployment: $10,000
  • Customer Support Integration: $20,000

Objectives and Scope

Objectives: Develop a mobile app that provides self-service options such as billing inquiries, account management, service troubleshooting, and live chat support, to improve customer satisfaction and reduce service costs.

Scope:

  • Included:
    • Design, development, testing, and deployment of the mobile app for iOS and Android platforms.
    • Integration with the companyâ€™s existing CRM system to enable customer service features such as live chat.
    • Implementation of core features: billing inquiries, account management, troubleshooting, and live chat support.
    • Compliance with security and accessibility standards (OWASP and WCAG).
  • Excluded:
    • Development of a web-based version of the application.
    • Customer service representative training on app usage.
    • Major overhauls of the existing CRM system beyond basic integration needs.

Success Criteria

  • App development and deployment are completed within budget and on time.
  • All features, including live chat and troubleshooting, are functional and user-friendly.
  • Customer satisfaction scores improve by at least 20% within the first quarter post-launch.
  • Customer service calls are reduced by 30% after app adoption.

Assumptions and Constraints

Assumptions

  • The app will comply with security and privacy standards.
  • All required tools and resources will be available.
  • Stakeholders will provide timely feedback during review phases.

Constraints

  • The budget is limited to $100,000.
  • The app must be deployed by March 31, 2025.
  • Integration with customer support systems must not exceed 20% of the timeline.

Roles and Responsibilities

  • Project Manager: Oversee all phases, manage budget, and ensure deadlines are met.
  • UI/UX Designer: Create an intuitive and engaging user interface.
  • Mobile App Developers: Implement front-end and back-end functionality.
  • QA Team: Test functionality, performance, and security.
  • Customer Support Specialist: Integrate app with CRM systems.

Communication Standards

Regular updates will be provided via bi-weekly team meetings and detailed progress reports. Stakeholders will receive milestone updates, and urgent issues will be communicated through email or messaging platforms.
