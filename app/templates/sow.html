Statement of Work (SOW)

Scope of Work

The project involves the development of a mobile application to enhance customer service for a telecommunications company. The app will include features such as billing inquiries, service troubleshooting, account management, and live chat support. This project encompasses the design, development, testing, and deployment phases, as well as integration with existing customer support systems.

Location of Work

The development team will work remotely, utilizing cloud-based collaboration tools for communication and version control. Testing and deployment will occur at the company's data center and app store platforms (Google Play Store and Apple App Store).

Period of Performance

The project will begin on December 3, 2024, and conclude on March 31, 2025. Key milestones include:

  • Requirement Gathering and Planning: December 3 - December 15, 2024
  • Design Phase: December 16, 2024 - January 15, 2025
  • Development Phase: January 16 - February 28, 2025
  • Testing and Deployment: March 1 - March 31, 2025

Deliverables Schedule

  • Requirement Specification Document: December 15, 2024
  • App Design Mockups: January 15, 2025
  • Fully Functional App: February 28, 2025
  • Testing and Bug Fixing Reports: March 15, 2025
  • Deployed App on App Stores: March 31, 2025

Applicable Standards

  • Data Privacy: Compliance with GDPR and CCPA regulations for customer data protection.
  • Security: OWASP Mobile Security standards to ensure secure app development.
  • Accessibility: Adherence to WCAG 2.1 standards for app usability.
  • App Store Guidelines: Compliance with Google Play and Apple App Store submission requirements.

Acceptance Criteria

  • The app must pass all functional, performance, and security tests.
  • All core features (billing, troubleshooting, account management, live chat) must work as specified.
  • The app must be successfully deployed to the Google Play Store and Apple App Store.
  • Positive feedback from internal stakeholders during the final review phase.

Special Requirements

  • The app must integrate seamlessly with the company's existing CRM system.
  • All customer data must be encrypted during transmission and storage.
  • High availability and scalability must be ensured to handle peak user loads.
  • The app must support both iOS and Android platforms.
